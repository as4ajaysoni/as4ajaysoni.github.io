Health Insurance

Critical — Medical Emergencies Can Wipe You Out
🏥

Why Health Insurance is Non-Negotiable

Medical emergencies can wipe out lifetime savings in days. A comprehensive health insurance policy with adequate coverage (₹10-25L for family) is essential. Look for no co-pay, restoration benefits, and coverage for pre-existing conditions.

Never skip health insurance. One hospitalization can cost ₹5-50L. Premiums are cheaper when you're younger and healthier.
📋

Types of Health Insurance

Individual health insurance covers one person. Family floater covers all family members under one sum insured. Senior citizen policies are for parents. Top-up plans add coverage above base policy at lower cost.

Family floater is cost-effective for families. But individual policies give independent coverage - consider both options.
✅

Key Features to Look For

Essential features: No co-pay, no sub-limits, restoration benefit, day care coverage, pre/post hospitalization, ambulance cover. Also check network hospitals, claim settlement ratio, and waiting period for pre-existing conditions.

Check: Claim settlement ratio >95%, cashless network hospitals near you, no hidden co-pay clauses.
💰

How Much Coverage Do You Need

Coverage depends on city, age, family size, and lifestyle. Metro cities need ₹15-25L minimum. Factor in inflation (medical costs rise 10-15%/year). Add ₹5L for critical illness rider.

Rule: Coverage = ₹5L + (₹2L × family members) + (₹5L × if in metro). Update coverage every 3-5 years.

Life Insurance

Protecting Your Family's Future
🏠

Term Life Insurance

Term life provides maximum coverage at minimum cost. If you have dependents, ensure coverage equals 10-15x your annual income. Pure term plans (no investment component) offer the best value.

Take term insurance for 30-40 year tenure. Cover until retirement age when dependents become self-sufficient.
💵

How Much Term Cover Do You Need

Calculate: Outstanding loans + 10 years of income replacement + children's education + family expenses. This ensures your family can maintain lifestyle without financial stress if you're not there.

Formula: Annual Income × Years to Retirement + Outstanding Debts + Goals Funding. Review annually.
🔄

Return of Premium vs Pure Term

Pure term is cheapest - you pay for coverage only. Return of premium refunds all premiums if you survive term. Mathematically, you're better off with pure term and investing the difference.

ROI term costs 2-3x pure term. Invest the difference in index funds. You'll likely come out ahead.
🎯

Accidental Death & Disability

Accidental death benefit pays extra if death is due to accident. Disability rider pays if you're permanently disabled. These are relatively cheap and provide crucial protection for breadwinners.

Add accidental death cover equal to term cover. Add total/partial permanent disability rider. Costs are minimal.
03

Property & Asset Insurance

Protecting Your Physical Assets
🏡

Home Insurance

Home insurance protects against fire, theft, natural disasters, and vandalism. Whether you own or rent, insurance is crucial. Building insurance covers structure, contents insurance covers belongings.

Home insurance: ₹5,000-₹15,000/year for ₹1 Cr cover. Don't skip - natural disasters can destroy everything.
🚗

Vehicle Insurance

Third-party insurance is mandatory. Comprehensive coverage adds protection for your own vehicle against theft, accidents, and damage. Zero-depreciation cover eliminates deduction for depreciation in claims.

Add zero-depreciation and engine protection. Premium increases slightly but saves big in claims.
🏢

Business Asset Insurance

For business owners, insure inventory, equipment, furniture, and premises. Business interruption insurance covers lost income during disruptions. Marine insurance for goods in transit.

Calculate: Replacement cost of all business assets + 6-12 months of revenue as business interruption cover.
📦

Travel & Transit Insurance

Travel insurance covers trip cancellation, lost baggage, medical emergencies abroad, and flight delays. Transit insurance protects valuable items during shipping or moving.

Always buy travel insurance for international trips. For domestic, assess based on trip cost and duration.
04

Specialized Insurance Covers

Often Overlooked But Critical
☔

Critical Illness Cover

Critical illness policies pay a lump sum on diagnosis of serious diseases (cancer, heart attack, stroke, kidney failure). This is different from health insurance - it's a pay-out to manage life after diagnosis.

Add critical illness cover of 5-10x annual income. Premium is small compared to potential payout.
♿

Disability Insurance

Disability cover replaces income if you're unable to work due to accident or illness. Many underestimate disability risk - it's more common than death before retirement age.

Disability income rider: 50-70% of income replacement. Most critical for self-employed and single earners.
✈️

Cyber Insurance

Cyber insurance protects against online fraud, identity theft, data breaches, and cyber extortion. Increasingly relevant as digital transactions grow. Covers financial losses and recovery costs.

Essential for those with significant online presence, business, or digital assets. Premium: ₹2,000-₹10,000/year.
🎓

Insurance for Children

Child plans provide education funding plus insurance protection. However, pure term on parent + PPF/index fund for education often works better. Evaluate before buying bundled child plans.

Don't buy insurance as investment. Buy term on parent, invest separately. Child plans have high costs and low returns.
